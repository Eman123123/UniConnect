About University

Welcome to NUST

National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST) is Pakistan's premier engineering and technology university, established in 1991. It's renowned for its cutting-edge research and academic excellence in STEM fields.

Ranked #1 university in Pakistan (QS Rankings)

Over 20,000 students across multiple campuses

More than 1,200 faculty members

16 schools offering 125+ degree programs
