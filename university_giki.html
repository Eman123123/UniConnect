About University

Welcome to GIK Institute

Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology (GIKI) is Pakistan's premier institution for engineering education, established in 1993. Located in Topi, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, it's renowned for its rigorous academic standards and research excellence.

Ranked among top 3 engineering universities in Pakistan

Over 2,000 undergraduate and graduate students

150+ faculty members with PhDs

5 engineering departments with 12 degree programs
